Be kind to yourself and avoid * Spaces in file names * Punctuation * Accented characters * Different files named `foo` and `Foo` --- ## Human readable * Name contains info on content * Connects to concept of a *slug* from semantic URLs --- ## Example **Which set of file(name)s do you want at 3 a.m. before a deadline?** ![human_readable_not_options]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/slide-shows/intro-file-naming/human_readable_not_options.png) --- class: center, middle .one-half[ ![slug_filenames]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/slide-shows/intro-file-naming/slug_filenames.png) ] .one-half[ ![slug]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/slide-shows/intro-file-naming/slug.jpg) ] --- ## Recap: Human readable Easy to figure out what something is, based on its name * Plays well with default ordering -
